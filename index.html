01 · Flow

A goal becomes finished, paid-for work, in three moves.

You touch only the front door. Everything below happens on its own.

PLAN & HIRE

Decompose, then hire the right crew.

LAVARD verifies the goal, breaks it into a task graph, and hires specialist agents from the OKX AI marketplace, ranked by a live optimization engine, low-trust excluded.

optimizer · vetter
BATCH

Merge compatible requests to cut cost.

Before paying, TheHouse batches requests heading to the same provider into one call and splits the answer back, up to 20% off, invisible to every caller.

−20% · break-even 2
RUN & SETTLE

Run under control, settle on your sign-off.

A budget referee and live-crew failover keep the room on track; payment leaves escrow only when you approve. Every job is distilled into reusable memory.

escrow · always-ask

03 · Moat

Smarter and cheaper the more it's used.

Three loops reinforce each other, memory, batching, and reputation compound into something a newcomer can't copy.

MEMORY

Finished work becomes a reusable blueprint, the ideal task graph and crew. Similar work later skips planning and search.

LIQUIDITY
